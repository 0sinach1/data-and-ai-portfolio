* Certain product categories contribute significantly more to total revenue despite lower sales volume.

* Monthly analysis shows noticeable fluctuations in sales, indicating seasonal patterns.

* Some product categories exhibit high demand variability, making them less predictable and harder to manage.

* Stable product categories show consistent sales across months, making them easier to plan for.
